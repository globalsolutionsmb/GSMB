About GSMB

Built on experience, relationships, and global manufacturing excellence.

Our Story

GSMB (Global Solutions Manufacturing Business) was founded by a veteran of the global supply chain and manufacturing industry, with extensive hands-on experience in international sourcing, procurement, and production management.

After years of working closely with manufacturers, project developers, and distributors across multiple continents, GSMB was established to help companies access reliable, high-quality production partners without unnecessary risk, complexity, or cost.

Today, GSMB supports clients worldwide by combining strategic sourcing expertise with strong operational execution and long-term relationship management.

Miki Benishay - Founder & CEO

Founded and led by an experienced international sourcing professional, GSMB is driven by deep industry knowledge and first-hand manufacturing expertise.

With years of direct involvement in factory operations, supplier negotiations, quality management, and logistics coordination, the company’s leadership ensures that every project meets the highest standards of performance and reliability.

This hands-on leadership approach enables GSMB to act as a true extension of its clients’ procurement and operations teams.

Our Global Network

GSMB operates with dedicated operational teams in China and India, providing on-the-ground support for supplier identification, audits, production monitoring, and quality control.

In parallel, GSMB maintains strong and long-standing partnerships with trusted manufacturers and commercial partners in the United States, Latin America, Turkey, Italy, Europe, and the Middle East.

This diversified sourcing footprint enables flexible, resilient, and region-optimized supply strategies for our clients.
