Our Services

Global sourcing and supply chain solutions tailored to your technical and commercial requirements.

Electrical Cables

GSMB specializes in sourcing low, medium, and high-voltage electrical cables, including copper conductor cables and solar PV cables for industrial, infrastructure, and renewable energy projects.

We work exclusively with qualified manufacturers complying with international standards, ensuring consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery.

PCB & PCBA (IPC Class 3)

We provide full-service sourcing for printed circuit boards and electronic assemblies, including IPC Class 3 PCBA for aerospace, medical, automotive, and industrial applications.

Capabilities include SMT, THT, functional testing, and full traceability.

Construction Steel & Solar Mounting

GSMB supplies structural steel for solar, infrastructure, and construction projects.

Profiles include IPE, UPN, W/H beams, and solar piles, compliant with international standards.

Industrial Mechanical Assemblies

We support OEM clients with sourcing of welded structures, machined components, sheet metal, and integrated assemblies.

Services include supplier qualification, inspections, and logistics coordination.

Worldwide Coverage & Strategic Sourcing

GSMB operates globally across North America, Europe, Latin America, Middle East, and Asia.

With teams in China and India and partners worldwide, we provide sourcing flexibility aligned with regulatory and geopolitical requirements.

Result: controlled risk, transparency, and optimized supply chains.
