Our Services

Global sourcing and supply chain solutions tailored to your technical and commercial requirements.

Electrical Cables

GSMB specializes in sourcing low, medium, and high-voltage electrical cables, including copper conductor cables and solar PV cables, for industrial, infrastructure, and renewable energy projects.

We work exclusively with qualified manufacturers that comply with international standards and certifications, ensuring consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery worldwide.

PCB & PCBA (Including Class 3)

GSMB provides full-service sourcing for printed circuit boards and electronic assemblies, including high-reliability IPC Class 3 PCBA for military, aerospace, automotive, medical, and industrial applications.

Our capabilities include advanced SMT and THT assembly, cleanroom production, functional testing, and full traceability, ensuring compliance with the most demanding technical and regulatory standards.

We support our clients throughout the entire process, from prototype to mass production, with strict supplier qualification and continuous quality monitoring.

Construction Steel & Solar Mounting Solutions

GSMB supplies high-quality structural steel for solar, infrastructure, and construction projects worldwide.

Our portfolio includes hot-dip galvanized IPE and UPN profiles, W and H beams, and solar mounting piles engineered for durability, structural integrity, and long-term outdoor performance.

We support EPC contractors, solar developers, and construction companies with flexible sourcing strategies, including both Chinese and non-Chinese origin options depending on project requirements.

Available steel grades include S235, S275, and S355, fully compliant with international standards.

Industrial Mechanical Assemblies

GSMB supports OEM and industrial clients with the sourcing and coordination of precision mechanical parts, fabricated components, and complete sub-assemblies.

Our capabilities include welded structures, structural brackets, machined components, sheet metal fabrication, and integrated mechanical assemblies built to international quality standards.

We manage supplier qualification, production supervision, quality control inspections, and logistics to ensure reliable integration into your manufacturing, infrastructure, or energy projects.

From prototype batches to large-scale production, we provide transparent communication, competitive pricing, and dependable execution.

Worldwide Coverage & Strategic Sourcing Advantage

GSMB operates on a truly global scale, supporting projects across North America, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, and Asia.

With operational teams in China and India and strong relationships with manufacturers in the USA, LATAM, Turkey, Italy, and other strategic markets, we provide sourcing flexibility tailored to your technical, regulatory, and geopolitical requirements.

For cost-driven projects, our long-established partnerships in China allow us to secure highly competitive pricing, optimized lead times, and reliable production capacity.

For projects requiring alternative origin, we leverage our international network to deliver compliant, high-quality solutions without sourcing limitations.

The result: transparent communication, controlled risk, and a supply chain strategy aligned with your commercial objectives.
