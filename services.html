Our Services

Global sourcing and supply chain solutions tailored to your technical and commercial requirements.

Electrical Cables

GSMB specializes in sourcing low, medium, and high-voltage electrical cables, including copper conductor cables and solar PV cables, for industrial, infrastructure, and renewable energy projects.

We work exclusively with qualified manufacturers that comply with international standards and certifications, ensuring consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery worldwide.

PCB & PCBA (Including Class 3)

GSMB provides full-service sourcing for printed circuit boards and electronic assemblies, including high-reliability IPC Class 3 PCBA for military, aerospace, automotive, medical, and industrial applications.

Our capabilities include advanced SMT and THT assembly, cleanroom production, functional testing, and full traceability, ensuring compliance with the most demanding technical and regulatory standards.

We support our clients throughout the entire process, from prototype to mass production, with strict supplier qualification and continuous quality monitoring.

Construction Steel

GSMB sources structural steel, profiles, and fabricated components for construction, infrastructure, and heavy industry projects.

We manage material specifications, certification requirements, and logistics coordination to ensure full compliance with project standards.

Mechanical Assemblies

We support the sourcing and coordination of precision-machined parts, sub-assemblies, and complete mechanical systems for OEM and industrial clients.

Our team supervises production, quality control, and shipment to ensure reliable integration into your manufacturing or construction processes.

Worldwide Coverage & China Advantage

All GSMB sourcing and procurement activities are provided on a global scale.

With operational teams in China and India and long-standing partnerships across North America, Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East, we deliver optimized pricing, stable supply chains, and strong manufacturing support.

Our long-term relationships with leading Chinese manufacturers allow us to secure some of the most competitive production conditions in the market, without compromising quality or compliance.
